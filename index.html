who am i?

Hello! My name is Cassie and I am a graphic designer based in Tri-Cities, Washington, and my passion is creating functional yet aesthetically pleasing designs.

I earned a Bachelors degree in Visual Communication Design and a Certificate in User Experience from Eastern Washington University in 2018. I am a 2019 finalist in the national AAF American Advertising Awards (The ADDYs, it's like the Grammy's for advertising!) after winning Silver in the District XI competition amongst other designers in Alaska, Idaho, Montana, Oregon, and Washington. I have over two years of professional design experience, mostly in promotional design and photography, and am proficient in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Premiere Pro, and After Effects.
